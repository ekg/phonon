Pattern: "bd bd bd bd sn ~ sn ~ hh ~ hh ~ hh ~ hh ~"

Timing:
- Each step = 125ms (16th note at 120 BPM)
- Full pattern = 2 seconds (one bar)
- 10 seconds = 5 repetitions

Sound Character:
● Kick (bd): Deep, punchy bass drum on every quarter note (four-on-the-floor)
● Snare (sn): Crisp snare hit on beats 2 and 4 (backbeat)
● Hi-Hat (hh): Bright, short hi-hat on off-beats for groove

This creates the classic house music groove:
BOOM BOOM BOOM BOOM (steady kick)
     SNAP     SNAP  (snare backbeat)
         TSS TSS TSS TSS (driving hi-hats)
